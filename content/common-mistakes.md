# ⚠️ Teen Patti Common Mistakes Beginners Must Avoid

## 🪶 Introduction

Teen Patti is easy to learn, but many beginners lose money quickly because of simple mistakes.

If you want to improve faster, you must first understand what **NOT to do**.

This guide covers the most common Teen Patti mistakes and how to avoid them.

---

## ❌ 1. Playing Too Many Hands

![Teen Patti player making the mistake of playing too many hands](../assets/images/common-mistakes-playing-too-many-hands.png)

One of the biggest beginner mistakes is:

👉 **Playing every hand**

### Why this is bad:
- Weak hands lose more often
- You waste chips quickly

### What to do instead:
✔ Play selectively
✔ Fold weak hands early

---

## ❌ 2. Ignoring Opponent Behavior

Teen Patti is not just cards — it's psychology.

### Mistake:
- Not paying attention to betting patterns

### Fix:
✔ Watch how others bet
✔ Identify aggressive or passive players

---

## ❌ 3. Betting Without a Strategy

Many beginners:

👉 Bet randomly without thinking

### Result:
- Lose chips fast
- Become predictable

### Better approach:
✔ Have a plan before betting
✔ Adjust based on situation

---

## ❌ 4. Overusing Bluffing

Bluffing is powerful, but:

👉 **Too much bluffing = easy to read**

### Mistake:
- Bluffing every round

### Fix:
✔ Bluff occasionally
✔ Choose the right timing

---

## ❌ 5. Poor Bankroll Management

This is one of the most dangerous mistakes.

### Mistake:
- Betting too much too quickly

### Fix:
✔ Set limits
✔ Control your spending

---

## ❌ 6. Playing Emotionally

Playing emotionally often leads to bad decisions.

### Signs:
- Chasing losses
- Getting frustrated
- Making risky bets out of anger

### Fix:
✔ Stay calm
✔ Take breaks when needed

---

## 🧠 Quick Tips to Avoid Mistakes

- Be patient
- Observe carefully
- Play fewer hands
- Think before betting

---

## 🧾 Summary

Avoiding mistakes is the fastest way to improve in Teen Patti.

🎯 Focus on:
- Discipline
- Observation
- Smart decision-making
